Mission Critical – Data Center

Hyperscale mission-critical data center in Tennessee, focused on reliability, redundancy and precise mechanical execution.

Mission-Critical • Data Center • Tennessee

This mission-critical data center project required tight coordination between design, field teams, and trade partners to deliver mechanical systems that meet strict uptime and performance requirements. I supported the construction management team across coordination, documentation, and site execution.

  • Supported mechanical construction for a mission-critical hyperscale data centre, ensuring reliability, redundancy, and compliance with strict performance standards.
  • Created, reviewed, and tracked mechanical hanger submittals, coordinating with engineering teams to ensure accuracy and timely approval.
  • Assisted field teams by updating installation plans, markups, and mechanical layout corrections using Bluebeam and project specifications.
  • Conducted routine site walks to verify hanger placement, material readiness, system routing, and conformance with project drawings.
  • Used OpenSpace to capture 360° site progress, enabling remote monitoring, issue identification, and documentation for leadership.
  • Coordinated with procurement and field supervision to align material deliveries, equipment needs, and daily work sequencing.
  • Tracked weekly labor productivity, crew performance, and task-level progress, helping identify delays and highlight resource needs.
  • Collaborated with project managers on RFI preparation, tracking logs, and document control, ensuring clear communication across teams.
  • Supported quality control efforts, ensuring mechanical installation met safety, alignment, and specification requirements.
  • Contributed to closeout workflows by organizing O&M documentation, equipment data, and mechanical system records for final turnover.
