Sorrento Rossette — 31-Story Twin Towers, Vadodara

Residential high-rise project using Tunnel Form Construction, where I supported planning, coordination, and quality workflows as an intern at UA Design.

Residential High-Rise • Vadodara • UA Design

Sorrento Rossette is one of Vadodara’s tallest residential developments, a 31-story twin-tower project delivered using high-speed Tunnel Form Construction. I worked alongside the project team to keep structural cycles efficient while maintaining accuracy and quality on site.

  • Contributed to the development of Vadodara’s tallest 31-story twin towers using Tunnel Form Construction, achieving a floor cycle every 80 hours.
  • Assisted with project planning, site coordination, material management, and quality control, working with AutoCAD, Revit, and Microsoft Project.
  • Ensured accurate formwork alignment using Total Station and laser leveling, and monitored concrete curing for early-strength performance.
  • Streamlined material supply through Just-in-Time (JIT) inventory management to maintain continuous workflow and minimize delays.
  • Addressed construction challenges—including alignment issues, logistics bottlenecks, labor shortages, and weather impacts—through structured planning and coordinated execution.
